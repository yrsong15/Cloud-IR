Project: Cloud IR

Hi! We're two college students working on Project: Cloud IR, a corporate data mining platform that produces visual pictograms of business keywords for leading corporations.

Our database is comprised of text from online sources including Twitter and Facebook, as well as reports released by the companies themselves. The data has been analyzed using R.

The site is still in its very early stages, but we will provide more updates as soon as possible. If you would like to reach out to us, please email Sintae or Ray!
